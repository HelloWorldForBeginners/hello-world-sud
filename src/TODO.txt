Monster generation

monsters will generate with a level within x levels of current player level
modifiers by monster type (defensive = higher def/hp, aggressive = higher attack, etc)
groups of rooms (i.e. all dungeon type rooms) will have a max monster cap
only certain monsters will be eligible to span in an area type
monsters may randomly move between rooms


Escaping combat
DONE

build in escape rolling
5 = escape no damage
3-4 = escape losing some money
1-2 = escape after monster attacks
0 = don't escape, and monster attacks


Randomize room layout within group

Rooms within a group will have randomized directions.
Certain directions will be forced
Certain rooms will not be allowed next to others
